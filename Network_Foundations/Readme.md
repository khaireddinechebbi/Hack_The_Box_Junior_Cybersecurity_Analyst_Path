# Description:
This module, explores the technology behind computer networking - also known as "networking" or "networks" - and why it is essential to our lives. and mostly focus on two primary types of networks: Local Area Networks (LANs) and Wide Area Networks (WANs).

And gives you an understanding of how devices are able to communicate with one another, from inside our homes to across the globe, is fundamental knowledge for those looking to enter the field of cyber security. The interconnectedness of almost every device in our world today sets the backdrop for the ever increasing demand for security professionals.
